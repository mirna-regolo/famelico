Library of Culinary Sources

Discover the books that have inspired our culinary project. Each work is more than just a cookbook—it is a window into the history of Italian cuisine, preserving traditions, techniques, and regional flavors. By exploring these sources, we delve into the foundations that have shaped our approach, drawing from the wisdom of past generations to celebrate and reinterpret Italy’s rich gastronomic heritage.

About

famelico /fa·mè·li·co/

adj. [from Latin famelĭcus, derived from fames "hunger"] (pl. m. -ci)

Being "famelici" means more than satisfying hunger—it’s a deep craving for knowledge, a relentless pursuit of discovery, and a commitment to preserving traditions, memories and wisdom.

What is Famelico?

A platform dedicated to the preservation and enhancement of Italian gastronomic culture.

Through an interactive system of ingredients, regions, recipes, and historical sources, famelico allows you to explore culinary roots, discover documented variants, and preserve traditional knowledge. By intertwining history, territory, and innovation, it forms a living archive that safeguards and passes on the heritage of Italian taste.
